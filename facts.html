Facts
  1. Poachers kill or capture animals to sell them locally or for the global trade in wildlife. This has many names, including animal poaching and animal trafficking.
  2. The ivory tusks of African elephants, for example, are carved into trinkets or display pieces.
  3. Poaching has devastating consequences for wildlife. In some instances, it’s the primary reason why an animal faces a risk of extinction.
  4. Wildlife trading is a major black market that has increased alongside rising wealth in Asia—a major consumer of wildlife
