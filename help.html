How to Help

Poaching negatively affects us and our environment. Fortunately, there are ways to slow the momentum poaching has. We can increase the effort needed for poachers to get what they want. If they do follow through with their plans the risk needs to increase. The consequences need to be less lenient on those who do these illegal crimes. Lastly, we need to reduce the rewards these poachers get for their items. Once they aren't getting a substantial amount of profit as they once would, they'll stop.
